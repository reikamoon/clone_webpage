    Why Azure Moon is the Best Route

    October 27, 2019 Mika FE Heroes, Breaking News 3 comments

    Azure Moon is hands down the best route in Fire Emblem Three Houses because the characters are very interesting and they all do their best, plus their lord is trying so hard, how can you not feel bad for the poor guy? Also you get to kill Arundel in this route, and it is the only route where you actually get to kill him yourself.
